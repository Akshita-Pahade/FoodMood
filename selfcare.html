Quinoa Chickpea Salad

A refreshing and protein-rich salad with quinoa, chickpeas, and fresh veggies tossed in lemon dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas
  • ½ cup chopped cucumber
  • ½ cup chopped tomatoes
  • ¼ cup chopped onions
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped coriander or parsley

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, mix quinoa, chickpeas, and chopped veggies.
  2. Add lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Toss well and garnish with herbs.
  4. Chill for a few minutes and serve fresh!
Vegetable Stir-Fry with Tofu

Colorful veggies stir-fried with tofu, garlic, ginger, and soy sauce. A quick and healthy dinner!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup firm tofu (cubed)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • Salt & pepper
  • Optional: sesame seeds

Steps:

  1. Heat oil, sauté garlic and ginger.
  2. Cook tofu until golden brown.
  3. Add veggies and stir-fry 4–5 mins.
  4. Add soy sauce, seasoning, stir and serve.
Banana Oat Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes made with bananas, oats, and eggs. Naturally sweet and perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • ½ cup oats
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup milk
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: cinnamon, vanilla

Steps:

  1. Mash banana and mix all ingredients.
  2. Form batter, cook on non-stick pan.
  3. Flip and serve with toppings.
Zucchini Noodles with Pesto

Zoodles tossed in pesto, a fresh and healthy dinner idea!

Ingredients:

  • 2 zucchinis spiralized
  • ¼ cup pesto
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic

Steps:

  1. Sauté garlic, add zoodles.
  2. Cook 2–3 mins, stir in pesto, serve.
Dark Chocolate Chia Pudding

Creamy chia pudding with dark chocolate. A perfect guilt-free dessert!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder

Steps:

  1. Mix all ingredients, refrigerate 2 hrs.
  2. Top with fruits or dark chocolate.
Cucumber Mint Detox Water

Cool and refreshing infused water for a healthy glow.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cucumber
  • 5–6 mint leaves
  • 1L water, lemon slices, ice

Steps:

  1. Add all to pitcher, refrigerate 1–2 hrs.
  2. Serve cold and enjoy!
